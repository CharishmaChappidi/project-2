Character Bios

Kalki 2898 AD

Bhairava: The main character, played by Prabhas, is a bounty hunter who wants to enter the Complex.

Ashwatthama: Played by Amitabh Bachchan, he is the son of Dronacharya.

Supreme Yaskin: Played by Kamal Haasan, he is a totalitarian god-king.

SUM-80 alias Sumathi: Played by Deepika Padukone, she is a pregnant lab subject.

Commander Manas: Played by Saswata Chatterjee, he is a commander of the Complex.

Counsellor Bani: Played by Anil George.

Kyra: Played by Anna Ben, she is a rebel from Shambhala.

Shobana as Mariam: She is the leader of Shambhala.

Pasupathy as Veeran: He is a rebel from Shambhala.

Keerthy Suresh as BU-JZ-1 alias Bujji: She is Bhairava's companion AI droid.

Stree 2

Vicky: Played by Rajkummar Rao, Vicky is a tailor who falls in love.

Rudra: Played by Pankaj Tripathi, Rudra is an expert in ancient texts.

Shamaa: Played by Tamannaah Bhatia, Shamaa is revealed to be Rudra's partner.

Janna: Played by Abhishek Banerjee, Janna is one of Vicky's loyal friends.

Bittu: Played by Aparshakti Khurana, Bittu is one of Vicky's loyal friends.

Sarkata: Played by Sunil Kumar, Sarkata is a headless monster.

Unnamed Woman: Played by Shraddha Kapoor, she plays a crucial role.

The Greatest of All Time

Gandhi: The main character, played by Vijay, is a hostage negotiator.

Rajiv Menon: The main antagonist, played by Mohan.

Jeevitha Gandhi: The daughter of Gandhi, played by Abyukta Manikandan.

Devara: Part 1

Devara: The village chief, played by N.T. Rama Rao Jr.

Vara: Devara's son, also played by N.T. Rama Rao Jr.

Bhaira: A violent chieftain, played by Saif Ali Khan.

Thangam: Vara's love interest, played by Janhvi Kapoor.

Singappa: A local storyteller, played by Prakash Raj.

Muruga: A politician-cum-smuggler, played by Murali Sharma.

Rayappa: Thangam's father, played by Srikanth.

Kora: Played by Shine Tom Chacko.

Irfan: A Coast Guard officer, played by Narain.

Kunjara: Kora's brother, played by Kalaiyarasan.

Hanu-Man

Hanumanthu: The main character, played by Teja Sajja.

Anjamma: Hanumanthu's older sister, played by Varalaxmi Sarathkumar.

Meenakshi: A doctor, played by Amritha Aiyer.

Michael: The antagonist, played by Vinay Rai.
